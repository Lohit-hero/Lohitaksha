About Me

Hey! I’m Lohitaksha — someone who deeply believes in personal growth, meaningful connection, and the incredible potential that lies within each of us. My mission is to inspire and empower people to live fully, dream boldly, and take intentional steps toward the futures they envision.

That mission first came to life with FIARD — Fostering Insight, Ambition, Resilience, and Development — the global coaching platform I founded to help professionals, entrepreneurs, and students strengthen their leadership, resilience, and mindset. At FIARD, I create personalized growth programs that combine evidence-based leadership strategies, neuroscience-backed mindset tools, and practical goal systems — guiding people toward lasting transformation and helping them discover just how much more they’re capable of.

It also found a home in Lohitaksha’s Den, my podcast — a space where curiosity drives the conversation. Through engaging discussions with guests from all walks of life, we explore leadership, personal development, philosophy, innovation, and the everyday wisdom that shapes how we live. Each episode is designed to spark reflection, inspire curiosity, and build a community that values learning and growth.

These experiences became the heart of my books — It’s Your Life: Make It Happen, a practical guide to living with clarity and purpose, and Brainwaves of the Bold, a deeper dive into transformative leadership and inner courage, encouraging readers to lead authentically and take bold action.

My academic journey has been equally meaningful — from serving as first author on a peer-reviewed publication to delivering presentations that create those unforgettable “light bulb” moments for others. These experiences have reinforced my belief in collaboration, perseverance, and the life-changing power of communication.

At the core of everything I do is a belief in the beauty of growth, the strength of human connection, and the limitless possibilities that open up when we believe in ourselves. Whether through coaching, writing, podcasting, speaking, or everyday conversations, I’m committed to creating spaces where authenticity, learning, and empowerment thrive.
