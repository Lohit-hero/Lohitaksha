About Me

Hey! I’m Lohitaksha — a passionate advocate for personal growth, meaningful connection, and the incredible potential that resides within each of us. My mission is to inspire and empower individuals to embrace life fully and take intentional steps toward creating the futures they envision.

That mission first came to life through FIARD (Fostering Insight, Ambition, Resilience, and Development), the global coaching platform I founded to help professionals, entrepreneurs, and students worldwide strengthen leadership, resilience, and mindset. At FIARD, I designed personalized growth programs blending evidence-based leadership strategies, neuroscience-backed mindset techniques, and practical goal systems — guiding clients to achieve lasting transformation and redefine what they believed possible.

It also took shape in Lohitaksha’s Den, my podcast — a dynamic space where curiosity leads the way. Through thoughtful conversations with diverse guests, we explore leadership, personal development, philosophy, innovation, and everyday wisdom. Each episode invites reflection, sparks curiosity, and fosters a community where learning is celebrated.

Both FIARD and my podcast became the foundation and inspiration for my books — It’s Your Life: Make It Happen, a practical and encouraging guide for navigating life with purpose and clarity, and Brainwaves of the Bold, a deep exploration of transformative leadership and inner courage that challenges readers to lead authentically and take bold action.

My academic journey has been equally meaningful — from leading a team as first author in publishing a peer-reviewed review article to delivering engaging presentations that ignite “light bulb” moments for others. These experiences have reinforced my belief in collaboration, perseverance, and the transformative power of communication.

At my core, I believe in the beauty of growth, the strength of human connection, and the limitless possibilities that unfold when we dare to believe in ourselves. Whether through coaching, writing, podcasting, speaking, or everyday conversations, I’m committed to fostering environments where authenticity, learning, and empowerment can thrive.
