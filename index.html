About Me

I’m an Integrated Master’s graduate (Chemical Sciences, University of Hyderabad) with a cross-disciplinary bend: computational/theoretical chemistry & physics, machine learning, and scientific communication. I love building clear mental models, writing precisely, and turning complex ideas into systems people actually use.

I founded FIARD—a leadership and mindset coaching platform—host the “Lohitaksha’s Den” podcast, and authored books on personal growth and leadership. On the research side, I led a peer-reviewed review article on eicosanoids in Alzheimer’s disease and completed a two-phase Master’s project on screened Coulomb potentials and confined hydrogen atoms (information-theoretic measures, plasma environments).
